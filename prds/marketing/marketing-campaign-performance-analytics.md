# PRD: Marketing Campaign Performance Analytics

## 1) Business context
**Goal / outcomes**
- Provide comprehensive analytics on marketing campaign performance to enable data-driven marketing decisions, optimize campaign spend, and measure ROI across all marketing channels

**Consumers & consumption modes**
- Marketing Managers
- Marketing Analysts
- Campaign Managers
- CMO
- Finance Team

## 2) Vulcan-first specification
**Grain**: campaign_id, date

**Entities**
- campaign: `campaign_id`
- channel: `channel_id`
- customer: `customer_id`
- product: `product_id`

**Primary time**: `date`

**Dimensions**
- campaign_id
- campaign_name
- channel
- channel_type
- campaign_type
- target_audience
- geography
- device_type
- customer_segment

**Measures**
- impressions
- clicks
- conversions
- revenue
- cost
- leads
- signups

**Metrics**
- **CTR**: Click-Through Rate: (clicks / impressions) * 100
- **Conversion Rate**: Percentage of clicks that result in conversions: (conversions / clicks) * 100
- **CPC**: Cost Per Click: cost / clicks
- **CPA**: Cost Per Acquisition: cost / conversions
- **ROAS**: Return on Ad Spend: revenue / cost
- **Revenue per Campaign**: Total revenue generated by a campaign
- **LTV by Campaign**: Customer Lifetime Value attributed to customers acquired through a specific campaign

## 3) Sources & lineage
